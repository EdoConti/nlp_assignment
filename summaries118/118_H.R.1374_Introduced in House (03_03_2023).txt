Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/03/2023)Ending COVID Vaccine Mandates for Colleges and Universities ActThis bill prohibits an institution of higher education (IHE) from receiving federal funds or participating in federal higher education programs if the IHE imposes a COVID-19 vaccine mandate.COVID-19 vaccine mandatemeans any requirement that an individual receive an immunization that is intended to prevent or mitigate COVID-19 as a condition of that individual's admission to, enrollment in, attendance at, or employment at an IHE.
