Shown Here:Introduced in House (01/12/2023)Emergency Savings Accounts Act of 2023This bill allows an individual taxpayer occupying a residence a deduction from gross income for up to $5,000 of amounts paid into such taxpayer's emergency savings account. The bill definesemergency savings accountas an account established exclusively to pay the qualified disaster and public health emergency expenses of the account beneficiary.The bill definesqualified disaster and public health emergency expensesas disaster mitigation expenses, disaster recovery expenses, public health emergency expenses, and unemployment-related expenses.
