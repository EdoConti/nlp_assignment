Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/15/2024)ThinkDIFFERENTLY About Disability Employment ActThis bill requires the Small Business Administration (SBA) to (1) provide assistance to individuals with disabilities who desire to become entrepreneurs or to be self-employed, (2) help individuals with disabilities find employment at small businesses; and (3) assist small businesses with hiring such individuals and with accessibility issues applicable to such individuals.The SBA must conduct outreach and education about such activities.The bill establishes reporting requirements with respect to these activities.
