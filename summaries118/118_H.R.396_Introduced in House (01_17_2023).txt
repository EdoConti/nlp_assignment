Shown Here:Introduced in House (01/17/2023)Closing the Bump Stock Loophole Act of 2023This bill subjects bump stocks to regulation under federal firearms laws.Specifically, the bill adds bump stocks to the list of firearms subject to regulation (e.g., registration and licensing requirements) under the National Firearms Act. Additionally, it includes bump stocks in the list of firearms subject to regulation (e.g., background check requirements) under the Gun Control Act of 1968.
