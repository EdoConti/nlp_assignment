Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/05/2023)Back the Blue Act of 2023This bill establishes new criminal offenses for killing, assaulting, and fleeing to avoid prosecution for killing a judge, law enforcement officer, or public safety officer.Additionally, the billexpands the list of statutory aggravating factors in death penalty determinations to also include the killing or attempted killing of a law enforcement officer, judge, prosecutor, or firefighter or other first responder;broadens the authority of federal law enforcement officers to carry firearms; andlimits federal court review of challenges to state court convictions for killing a public safety officer or judge.
