Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/20/2023)Railroad Employee Equity and Fairness Act or the REEF ActThis bill permanently exempts payments made from the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Account from sequestration. Sequestration is a process of automatic, usually across-the-board spending reductions under which budgetary resources are permanently cancelled to enforce specific budget policy goals.Currently, this exemption ends 30 days after the termination of the COVID-19 national emergency period.
