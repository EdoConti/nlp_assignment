Shown Here:Introduced in House (06/16/2023)Multiple Firearm Sales Reporting Modernization Act of 2023This bill expands reporting requirements with respect to sales or dispositions of multiple firearms.Currently, a federal firearms licensee (e.g., a gun dealer) must report multiple sales or dispositions of pistols or revolvers to the same person within five business days. This bill requires a federal firearms licensee to report multiple sales or dispositions of any firearms to the same person within five business days.
