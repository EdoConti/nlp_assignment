Shown Here:Introduced in House (07/27/2023)Tech to Save Moms ActThis bill supports the use of technology to improve, and address disparities in, maternal health outcomes.Specifically, the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Innovation may test telehealth models to screen and treat common pregnancy-related complications for Medicaid enrollees.In addition, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) must award grants toevaluate and expand the use of technology-enabled collaborative learning models by entities that provide services to pregnant and postpartum individuals in medically underserved areas or from certain populations, andreduce racial and ethnic disparities in maternal health outcomes by increasing access to digital tools.HHS must also contract with the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine to study the use of technology in maternal health care.
