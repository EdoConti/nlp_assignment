Shown Here:Reported to House (09/12/2024)Telehealth Enhancement for Mental Health Act of 2024 or the TELEMH Act of 2024This bill requires the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) to create a billing modifier for certain telehealth services under Medicare.Specifically, the CMS must create a billing modifier for telehealth services that are billed as incident to a physician or practitioner's services.
