Shown Here:Introduced in House (01/25/2023)Reducing Environmental Barriers to Unified Infrastructure and Land Development Act of 2023 Act or the REBUILD Actof 2023This bill sets forth a process to authorize states to assume the environmental review responsibilities of federal agencies. Specifically, it allows states to oversee the environmental review required under certain federal environmental laws for projects funded by, carried out by, or subject to approval by federal agencies.
