Shown Here:Introduced in House (06/15/2023)Lifting Immigrant Families Through Benefits Access Restoration Act or the LIFT the BAR ActThis bill repeals certain limits on the eligibility of noncitizens who lawfully reside in the United States for federal means-tested programs, including Medicaid, the Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP), programs funded through Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) grants, Supplemental Security Income (SSI), and certain nutrition and housing programs.
