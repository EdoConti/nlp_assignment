Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/18/2023)Forbidding the Increase of Rogue Enforcers Act or the FIRE ActThis bill prohibits hiring and related activities within the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (ATF).Specifically, the bill prohibits within the ATFnew appointments to existing positions,the establishement of new positions,new assignments of duties, andassignments, details, or transfers to new duty stations.
