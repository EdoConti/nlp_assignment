Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/10/2023)This bill requires the Department of Defense (DOD) to certify specified roads under the Defense Access Roads Program, which provides for the construction and maintenance of roads important to the national defense. Specifically, DOD must certify specified roads in the vicinity of (1) Beale Air Force Base, California, (2) Travis Air Force Base, California, and (3) Sierra Army Depot, California.
