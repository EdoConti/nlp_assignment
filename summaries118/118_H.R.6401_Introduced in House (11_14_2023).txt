Shown Here:Introduced in House (11/14/2023)Upskilling and Retraining Assistance ActThis bill increaes to $12,000 in 2024-2025, the exclusion from employee gross income, for income tax purposes, of employer-paid educational assistance programs.The bill also expands the exclusion to include amounts paid for education-related tools and technology (e.g., hand tools, computers, software, licensure fees).
