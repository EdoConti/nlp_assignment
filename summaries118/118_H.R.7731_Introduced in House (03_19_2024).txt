Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/19/2024)Growing Opportunities in Agriculture Act or the GO Ag ActThis bill directs the Department of Education to award competitive grants to eligible entities (e.g., local educational agencies) to create new agricultural education programs in secondary schools.Agricultural educationrefers to career and technical education that is focused on agriculture, including classroom or laboratory instruction, work-based learning, and leadership education that is delivered through career and technical student organizations.
