Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/13/2023)Honoring Vocational Education ActThis bill requires the Bureau of the Census to ensure that the Current Population Survey provides for a separate category of educational attainment for Postsecondary Nondegree Awardees.Postsecondary Nondegree Awardeeis defined as an individual whohas received a high school diploma or its recognized equivalent and a recognized postsecondary credential; andhas not received an associate, bachelor's, master's, graduate, or professional degree.
