Shown Here:Introduced in House (08/25/2023)America Grows Act of 2023This bill permanently funds several agencies that perform agriculture research.The bill provides specified funding for the following agencies within the Department of Agriculture:the Agricultural Research Service,the Economic Research Service,the National Agricultural Statistics Service, andthe National Institute of Food and Agriculture.The bill exempts the funding from sequestration, which is a process of automatic, usually across-the-board spending reductions under which budgetary resources are permanently cancelled to enforce specific budget policy goals.It also exempts the budgetary effects of the funding from the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010 (PAYGO) and the Senate PAYGO rule.
