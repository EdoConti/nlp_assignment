Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/29/2023)Pause for Gun Safety ActThis bill establishes a seven-day waiting period for firearm transfers to unlicensed individuals.Specifically, the bill makes it unlawful to transfer a firearm to an unlicensed individual unless seven calendar days have elapsed since the transferee's most recent offer to take possession of the firearm. A violator is subject to criminal penalties—a fine, up to five years in prison, or both.The seven-day waiting period does not apply to certain firearm transfers, such as a temporary transfer for use exclusively at a shooting range.
