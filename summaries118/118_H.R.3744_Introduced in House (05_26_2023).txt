Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/26/2023)Avionic Students Housing ActThis bill allows an airport, school, or other airport operator to provide housing on the non-aeronautical area grounds of an airport for students enrolled in an accredited avionic program. The bill defines anavionic programas leading to a degree, certification, or qualifications for a career as a pilot, mechanic, air traffic controller, airline support personnel, or other avionics-related field.
