Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/27/2023)Drone Act of 2023This bill creates a federal statutory framework to criminalize various conduct involving the misuse of drones.Among its provisions, the bill generally prohibits theoperation of a drone that poses an imminent safety hazard to vessels or motor vehicles;weaponization of a drone (e.g., attaching a firearm or explosive); andoperation of a drone that interferes with a law enforcement, emergency response, or military operation or activity of the federal government or of a state, local, or tribal government.
