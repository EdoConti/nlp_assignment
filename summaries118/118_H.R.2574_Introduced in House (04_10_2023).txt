Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/10/2023)EMS Counts ActThis bill requires the Department of Labor to revise the broad description under the occupational series33-2011 Firefightersof the 2018 Standard Occupational Classification System of the Bureau of Labor Statistics to separately account for firefighters who also serve as paramedics or emergency medical technicians (EMTs). Specifically, the classification system must include the following detailed occupations:Firefighters;Firefighter/EMTs;Firefighter/Paramedics; andFirefighters, All Other.
