Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/01/2023)Prostate Cancer Community Assistance, Research and Education Act of 2023 or the PC-CARE ActThis bill establishes a committee to develop, implement, and periodically revise a plan to coordinate federal activities and programs concerning prostate cancer, including those related to research, screening, diagnosis, and treatment.Committee members must include representatives from specified federal agencies, as well as researchers, clinicians, individuals living with prostate cancer (or their caregivers), and other stakeholders. In addition, at least half of the members must be physicians.
