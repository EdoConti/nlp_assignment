Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/30/2023)Strengthening Supply Chains Through Truck Driver Incentives Act of 2023This bill allows certain licensed commercial truck drivers a $7,500 tax credit in a taxable year. The adjusted gross income of such drivers may not exceed $90,000 ($135,000 in the case of a joint tax return and $112,500 for heads of household). Such drivers must have driven a truck in a trade or business for a specified number of hours in a taxable year.The credit expires after December 31, 2024.
