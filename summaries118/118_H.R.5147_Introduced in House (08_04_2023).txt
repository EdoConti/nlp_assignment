Shown Here:Introduced in House (08/04/2023)Investing in Veteran Entrepreneurial Talents Act or the Investing in VETS ActThis bill raises from 3% to 5% the federal contracting participation goal for small businesses that are owned and controlled by service-disabled veterans.Only small businesses owned and controlled by service-disabled veterans that have been certified by the Small Business Administration may be included in calculating goals related to small businesses owned and controlled by service-disabled veterans.
