Shown Here:Introduced in House (10/13/2023)Robin Danielson Menstrual Product and Intimate Care Product Safety Act of2023This bill requires the National Institutes of Health to conduct or support research on the extent to which components (including contaminants and substances used as fragrances, colorants, dyes, and preservatives) in menstrual products and intimate care products pose health risks to people who use the products or to the children of people who use the products during or before pregnancy.
