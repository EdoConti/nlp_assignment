Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/01/2023)Curb Leaders from Electioneering with Autographs on National Checks Act or as the CLEAN Checks ActThis bill prohibits certain names and likenesses on federal government checks.Specifically, any check issued by the federal government may not be signed by, or otherwise bear the name, signature, image, or likeness of, the President, the Vice President, any elected official or cabinet level officer of the United States, or any person who is a relative of such individual.
