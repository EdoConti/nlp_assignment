Shown Here:Introduced in House (06/27/2023)Equal Access to Reproductive Care ActThis bill includes assisted reproduction as a tax deductible medical expense. It definesassisted reproductionas any methods, treatments, procedures, and services for effectuating a pregnancy and bringing it to term, and treats it as medical care of the taxpayer, the taxpayer's spouse, or a dependent to the extent that they intend to take legal custody or responsibility for any children born as a result of such assisted reproduction.
