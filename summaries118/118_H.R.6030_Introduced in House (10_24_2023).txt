Shown Here:Introduced in House (10/24/2023)Expanding Health Care Options for First Responders ActThis bill establishes a Medicare buy-in option for certain qualifying first responders.Specifically, the bill allows first responders aged 50 to 64 to enroll in Medicare if they are retired or otherwise separated from service due to a disability. The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) must determine enrollment periods and set premiums for the buy-in option established under the bill, in accordance with specified requirements. The CMS must also award grants to states and nonprofit organizations for outreach and enrollment activities relating to the buy-in option.
