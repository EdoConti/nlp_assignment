Shown Here:Passed House (06/05/2023)Fair Investment Opportunities for Professional Experts ActThis bill expands the eligibility criteria for anaccredited investorfor purposes of participating in private offerings of securities to include an individual determined by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) to have qualifying professional knowledge through educational or professional experience. (Certain unregistered securities may only be offered to accredited investors.)The SEC is directed to revise the definition ofaccredited investorin Regulation D (which exempts certain offerings from SEC registration requirements) to conform to changes in this bill.
