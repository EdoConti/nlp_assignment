Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/27/2023)Equine Tax Fairness ActThis bill modifies the tax treatment of gains and losses from the sale of depreciable property used in a trade or business to eliminatehorsesfrom the definition oflivestock(thus making the 24-month holding period requirement for livestock inapplicable to horses and allowing horses to be treated as capital assets subject to the existing 1-year holding period requirement for long-term capital gains).
