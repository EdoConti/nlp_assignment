Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/19/2023)Keep Our Promise to America's Children and Teachers Act or the Keep Our PACT ActThis bill provides funding through FY2033 for grant programs operated by local educational agencies to provide supplementary educational and related services to low-achieving students and other students who attend elementary and secondary schools with relatively high concentrations of students from low-income families. Additionally, the bill permanently reauthorizes the grant program to assist states and outlying areas in providing special education and related services to children with disabilities.
