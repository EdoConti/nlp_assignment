Shown Here:Reported to House (03/12/2024)Puyallup Tribe of Indians Land Into Trust Confirmation Act of2023This bill takes approximately 17.264 acres of specified lands in Pierce County, Washington, into trust for the benefit of the Puyallup Tribe of the Puyallup Reservation. Lands taken into trust shall be part of the tribe's reservation.The United States shall not be liable for any environmental contamination that occurred on or before the date on which the land is taken into trust.Further, the bill prohibits gaming on the land taken into trust.
