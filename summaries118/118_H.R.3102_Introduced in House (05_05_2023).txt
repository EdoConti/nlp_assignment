Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/05/2023)Traditional Screening Application Act or theTSA ActThis bill prohibits the Transportation Security Administration (TSA) from using theXgender designation in the TSA PreCheck advanced security program and instead authorizes the TSA to allow program applicants and participants to select from onlyMaleorFemale. The bill defines the termsmaleandfemaleas the biological classification of male and female sex in the context of reproductive potential or capacity, as indicated by sex chromosomes, naturally occurring sex hormones, gonads, and nonambiguous internal and external genitalia present at birth.
