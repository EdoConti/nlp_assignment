Shown Here:Introduced in House (07/19/2023)Stop Executive Capture of Banking Regulators ActThis bill requires banking and financial regulatory entities to report to Congress prior to implementing a non-binding recommendation from the Financial Stability Oversight Council or contained in an Executive Order. This requirement applies to the Board Of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, the National Credit Union Administration, and the Federal Housing Finance Administration.
