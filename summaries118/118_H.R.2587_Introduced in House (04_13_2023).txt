Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/13/2023)Noncontiguous Shipping Reasonable Rate Act of 2023This bill provides that a rate for service in noncontiguous domestic ocean trade  is reasonable if such rate is within 10% of a rate set by a comparable international ocean rate index recognized by the Federal Maritime Commission. (Under current law, a rate is required to be reasonable, and the Surface Transportation Board generally has the authority to determine whether certain rates are reasonable.)
