Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/06/2023)Retirement Forfeiture for Members of Congress Turned Foreign Lobbyists ActThis bill prohibits a former Member of Congress or former senior congressional employee who is a registered lobbyist representing a foreign principal from being eligible forthe Civil Service Retirement System or the Federal Employees Retirement System, including the Thrift Savings Plan;the Federal Employees Health Benefits Program; orthe Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance Program.
