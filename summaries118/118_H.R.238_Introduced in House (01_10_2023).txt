Shown Here:Introduced in House (01/10/2023)Residential Substance Use Disorder Treatment Act of 2023This bill revises and reauthorizes through FY2027 grants for residential substance use disorder treatment programs at state and local correctional and detention facilities.Among the revisions, the billreplaces statutory references tosubstance abusewithsubstance use disorder,specifies that the termresidential substance use disorder treatment programincludes a medication-assisted treatment program,requires the chief medical officer or other staff overseeing a program to complete training on the science of addiction and the latest research and clinical guidance on treating substance use disorders in criminal justice settings, andallows people who are awaiting trial or in pretrial detention to participate in the programs.
