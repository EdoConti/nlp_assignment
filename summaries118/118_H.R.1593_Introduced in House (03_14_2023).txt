Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/14/2023)Land and Water Conservation Fund Water Amendments Act of 2023This bill authorizes the Department of the Interior to provide financial assistance for water quality improvement projects from amounts made available under the Land and Water Conservation Fund.Interior shall only provide such financial assistance to projects that seek to improve water quality by improving, restoring, remediating, or developing natural hydrological systems, such as wetlands or living shorelines.To be eligible for assistance, a state's comprehensive statewide outdoor recreation plan shall identifyany body of water within the state for which a water quality control plan has been developed pursuant to the Federal Water Pollution Control Act, andany proposed water quality project to be conducted with respect to such body of water.
