Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/20/2023)Prevent Family Fire Act of 2023This bill allows through 2030 a new tax credit equal to 10% of amounts received from the first retail sale of a safe firearm storage device, not to exceed $400 per device.The bill definessafe firearm storage deviceas a device that is designed and marketed to deny unauthorized access to, or render inoperable, a firearm or ammunition, and is secured by a combination lock, key lock, or lock based on biometric information.
