Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/10/2023)National Flood Insurance Program Affordability ActThis bill requires the Federal Emergency Management Agency to provide discounts on National Flood Insurance Program coverage to low-income households, small businesses, and not-for-profit entities. Specifically, the chargeable premium rate for an eligible primary residential dwelling and related personal property must not exceed 1% of the median income of the area in which the property is located.
