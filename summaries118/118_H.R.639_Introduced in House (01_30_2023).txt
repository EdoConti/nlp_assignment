Shown Here:Introduced in House (01/30/2023)Korematsu-Takai Civil Liberties Protection Act of 2023This bill prohibits the detention or imprisonment of an individual based solely on an actual or perceived protected characteristic of the individual. The termprotected characteristicincludes each of the following: race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, and any additional characteristic that the Department of Justice determines to be a protected characteristic.
