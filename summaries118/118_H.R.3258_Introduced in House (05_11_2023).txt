Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/11/2023)Targeting Resources for Equitable Access to Treatment for Long COVID Act or the TREAT Long COVID ActThis bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to award competitive grants to create or expand the capacity of clinics that treat patients with persistent, long-term symptoms following recovery from acute COVID-19 (i.e., Long COVID) and associated conditions. Eligible recipients include federally qualified health centers, rural health clinics, urban Indian health centers, and state and local health departments.
