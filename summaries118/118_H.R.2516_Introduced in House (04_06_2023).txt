Shown Here:Introduced in House (04/06/2023)End the Congressional Revolving Door ActThis bill prohibits a former Member of Congress or former senior congressional employee who is a registered lobbyist, and entitled to compensation as such, from being eligible for any benefits beginning after enactment of this bill underthe Civil Service Retirement System (CSRS), including the Thrift Savings Plan (TSP);the Federal Employees Retirement System (FERS), including the TSP;the Federal Employees Health Benefits Program, including federal enhanced dental and vision benefits; orthe Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance Program.The bill does not prevent the payment of any CSRS or FERS lump-sum credit to which an individual is entitled or any nonforfeitable amount in an individual's TSP account.
