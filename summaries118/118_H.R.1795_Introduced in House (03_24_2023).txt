Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/24/2023)Homecare for Seniors ActThis bill allows tax-exempt distributions from health savings accounts (HSAs) to be used for qualified home care.Qualified home caremeans a contract to provide three or more of the following services in the residence of the service recipient:assistance with eating,assistance with toileting,assistance with transferring,assistance with bathing,assistance with dressing,assistance with continence, andmedication adherence.The Department of Health and Human Services must carry out a campaign to increase public awareness of the in-home service expenses that are eligible for tax-free distribution from HSAs.
