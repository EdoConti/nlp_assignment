Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/10/2023)Jobs and Opportunities for Medicaid ActThis bill establishes work requirements under Medicaid for able-bodied adults.Specifically, the bill requires individuals who are between the ages of 18 and 65 and who are not otherwise unable to work due to a medical condition, family situation, or other listed reason to work or volunteer at least 20 hours per week, based on a monthly average, in order to qualify for Medicaid.
