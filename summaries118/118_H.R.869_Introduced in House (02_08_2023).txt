Shown Here:Introduced in House (02/08/2023)Revoking Entry Granted to Iranian Mullahs and Elites Act of 2023 or the REGIME Act of 2023The bill requires the Department of State to conduct a review of certain Iranian officials and government employees, as well as their immediate family members, who currently have visas or who have applied for visas. If such individuals are determined to be ineligible for entry into the United States under specified laws, the State Department must revoke their visas.
