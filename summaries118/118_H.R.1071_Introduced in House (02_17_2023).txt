Shown Here:Introduced in House (02/17/2023)Safe and Quiet Skies Act of 2023This bill sets forth requirements for commercial air tour flights.Among other requirements, the billprohibits tour flights within a half mile of military installations, national cemeteries, national wilderness areas, national parks, and national wildlife refuges;prohibits tour flights from operating at an altitude of less than 1,500 feet;requires tour flights over occupied areas (e.g., residential, commercial, and recreational areas) to be no louder than 55dbA; andallows states and localities to impose additional requirements that are stricter than the minimum federal requirements.
