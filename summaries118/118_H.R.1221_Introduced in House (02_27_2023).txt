Shown Here:Introduced in House (02/27/2023)Buffalo Tract Protection ActThis bill withdraws specified Bureau of Land Management lands in Placitas, New Mexico, from (1) location, entry, and patent under the mining laws; and (2) disposition under the mineral leasing, mineral materials, and geothermal leasing laws.Any conveyance of the surface estate of such federal land shall require a reservation of the mineral estate to the United States.
