Shown Here:Reported to House (07/30/2024)Women and Lung Cancer Research and Preventive Services Act of 2024This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to conduct an interagency review of lung cancer research, prevention, and awareness with respect to women and underserved populations.The review mustreport on the status of existing research and current knowledge gaps;identify new opportunities for collaborative research to determine the causes of lung cancer and advance prevention, screening, diagnosis, and treatment; andprovide recommendations for a national lung cancer screening strategy and public education campaign.
