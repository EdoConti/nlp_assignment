Shown Here:Introduced in House (01/09/2023)Help Ensure Legal Detainers Act or the HELD ActThis bill prohibits the use of federal funds by a state or local subdivision that has in effect a law, policy, or procedure that prevents or impedesa timely response to a request by the Department of Homeland Security for information about an alien in custody, including the alien's estimated release date; orcompliance with a detainer request.
