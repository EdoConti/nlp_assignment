Shown Here:Introduced in House (07/18/2023)This bill directs the Department of Agriculture to contract with the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine to study a federal research agenda to advance the understanding of food animal microbiomes (including cattle, small ruminants, poultry, and aquaculture species). The termmicrobiomegenerally refers to a community of microorganisms (such as bacteria, fungi, and viruses) that inhabit a particular environmentThe National Academies must submit a report to Congress on the study, including recommendations to advance the federal research agenda on food animal microbiomes.
