Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/15/2023)Farm Operations Support ActThis bill freezes the minimum wage for H-2A (temporary agricultural workers) through December 31, 2023.Specifically, the adverse effect wage rate that was in effect in a state on December 1, 2022, shall remain in effect through December 31, 2023.(Generally, the minimum wage for an H-2A worker is the highest of the adverse effect wage rate, the prevailing hourly wage or piece rate, the federal minimum wage, or the applicable state minimum wage.)
