Shown Here:Introduced in House (06/23/2023)Route 66 National Historic Trail Designation ActThis bill amends the National Trails System Act to designate a trail of approximately 2,400 miles extending from Chicago, Illinois, to Santa Monica, California, as the Route 66 National Historic Trail.The trail must be administered by the National Park Service in a manner that respects and maintains its idiosyncratic nature.The Department of the Interior may not use eminent domain or condemnation in carrying out this bill.
