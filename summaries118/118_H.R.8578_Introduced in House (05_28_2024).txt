Shown Here:Introduced in House (05/28/2024)Retreaded Truck Tire Jobs, Supply Chain Security and Sustainability Act of 2024This bill allows a new tax credit through 2027 for truck fleet retreaded tires. The amount of the credit shall be equal to the lesser of 30% of the basis of each qualified retreaded tire, or the amount incurred by a taxpayer to purchase such tires not exceeding $30 per tire.Aqualified retreaded tireis a retreaded truck tire that is retreaded and purchased in the United States.
