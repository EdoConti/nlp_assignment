Shown Here:Passed House (02/05/2024)Wildlife Innovation and Longevity Driver Reauthorization Act or the WILD ActThis bill reauthorizes through FY2028 and revises several conservation programs for wildlife species and their habitats, including the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service's Partners for Fish and Wildlife Program as well as programs that provide financial assistance for African elephants, Asian elephants, rhinoceroses, tigers, great apes, and marine turtles.
