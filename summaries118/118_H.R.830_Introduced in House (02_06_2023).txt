Shown Here:Introduced in House (02/06/2023)Help Ensure Lower Patient Copays Act or the HELP Copays ActThis bill requires health insurance plans to apply certain payments made by, or on behalf of, a plan enrollee toward a plan's cost-sharing requirements. Specifically, plans must apply third-party payments, financial assistance, discounts, product vouchers, and other reductions in out-of-pocket expenses toward the requirements.
