Shown Here:Passed House (05/15/2024)Detain and Deport Illegal Aliens Who Assault Cops ActThis bill requires the Department of Homeland Security to take custody of any non-U.S. national (alienunder federal law) who meets certain inadmissibility criteria and is arrested for, is charged with, is convicted of, or admits to assaulting a law enforcement officer, firefighter, or other first responder.The bill also directs the Government Accountability Office to report to Congress on the number of non-U.S. nationals detained under this bill.
