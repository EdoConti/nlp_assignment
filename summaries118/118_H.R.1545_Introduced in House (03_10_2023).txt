Shown Here:Introduced in House (03/10/2023)Restoring Competitive Property Insurance Availability ActThis bill excludes from the gross income of specified insurance companies (other than life insurance companies) certain income from providing qualified real property insurance after a federally declared disaster. The bill definesqualified real property insurance incometo mean the excess of premiums received by such insurance companies for real property insurance for property located in the disaster area, over deductions properly allocable to such premiums.
