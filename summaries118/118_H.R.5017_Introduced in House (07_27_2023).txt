Shown Here:Introduced in House (07/27/2023)Space Infrastructure ActThis bill directs the Department of Homeland Security to designate space systems, services, and technology as a critical infrastructure sector. Under current law,critical infrastructuremeans systems and assets, whether physical or virtual, so vital to the United States that the incapacity or destruction of such systems and assets would have a debilitating impact on security, national economic security, national public health or safety, or any combination of those matters.
