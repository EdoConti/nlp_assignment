Shown Here:Introduced in House (06/22/2023)Helping Emergency Responders Overcome Actor the HERO ActThis bill establishes a series of programs relating to the behavioral health of law enforcement officers, first responders, 9-1-1 operators, and other public safety officers and health care providers.The programs includea public safety officer suicide-reporting system at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention,a grant program for peer-support behavioral health and wellness programs within fire departments and emergency medical services agencies, anda grant program for behavioral health and wellness programs for health care providers.
